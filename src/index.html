EPTS tutors wanted

EPTS is Recruiting an ECSE and COMP tutor for the upcoming semester! Tutors are responsible for holding 3 hours of tutoring a week as well as hosting exam review sessions and are compensated $20 / hour. The deadline to apply is September 11th. For additional details about the service as well as different positions visit our website at http://epts.mcgilleus.ca. To apply for a position please email a copy of your CV and unofficial transcript to epts@mcgilleus.ca.

PERSWADE

We are pleased to invite outstanding eligible students to apply for enrolment in the PERSWADE Program.

PERSWADE aims at providing enhanced training opportunities to undergraduate and graduate students as well as Postdoctoral Fellows (PDFs) in the areas of wireless networks and systems (WNS) and their applications. More specifically, PERSWADE aims at improving their job readiness by instilling a well-gauged industrial orientation in their training experience.

Additional information on the program can be found on their website. All applications must be submitted by email directly to yang.chen@emt.inrs.ca no later than September 7, 2017.

To send a blurb, contact ecsess.vpcomm@mcgilleus.ca
