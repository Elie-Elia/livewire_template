McGill Rocket Team is Recruiting

The McGill Rocket Team creates reusable rockets and scientific experiments/payloads that fly up to 30,000 feet. Our systems are entirely developed in Montreal and launched annually at international launch facilities. It is an amazing opportunity to learn and meet interesting people. To carry this project forward, we are looking for ambitious students to take on technical and administrative roles. We need people from all backgrounds, not just engineering/science, and there are a variety of positions available.

For more details, come to our info session Thursday, September 14th. Link.

Applications are open to all students and are due Friday, September 15th. Link.
